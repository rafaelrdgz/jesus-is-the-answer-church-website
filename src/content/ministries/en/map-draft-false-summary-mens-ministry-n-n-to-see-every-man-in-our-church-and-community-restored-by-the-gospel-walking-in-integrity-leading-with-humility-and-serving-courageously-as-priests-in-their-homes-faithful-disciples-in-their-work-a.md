Men's Ministry Mission

"To gather, disciple, and mobilize men toward a reformed and practical faith in Jesus Christ through in-depth Scripture study, accountability, persistent prayer, and concrete service, so that every man recognizes that Jesus alone is the answer to his struggles, responsibilities, and purposes, and lives as an agent of grace and righteousness in his family, church, and society."

 Specific actions that reflect the objective:

1. Teach Reformed theology applied to men's lives – Addressing topics such as work, marriage, fatherhood, sexual purity, anger management, and servant leadership.

2. Foster accountability in small groups – Creating safe spaces where men are encouraged to confess their sins, pray for one another, and grow in holiness.

3. Develop leadership skills – Reminding men that they are called to be gentle yet firm heads, not through authoritarianism but through sacrificial love, reflecting Christ.

4. Promote practical service – Organizing maintenance projects, assisting widows, discipling young men, and providing logistical support to the church.
