Vision of the Evangelism Ministry

"To see every member of our church become a joyful witness to the Gospel, boldly and tenderly proclaiming that Jesus Christ is the only answer to humanity's guilt, pain, and hope, reaching our community and beyond with the transforming truth of God's sovereign grace, for His glory."

Biblical Foundation: Acts 1:8; Romans 10:13-15; 2 Corinthians 5:18-20.


Objective (Mission) of the Evangelism Ministry

"To equip, motivate, and mobilize the church to share the Gospel of Christ clearly, faithfully, and in context, through basic apologetics training, the organization of local mission outreaches, and accompaniment of new believers, so that every person within our reach recognizes that only Jesus is the answer for their life and submits to Him as Lord and Savior."

Concrete actions that reflect the objective:

1. Equipping believers in presenting the Gospel – Teaching biblical summaries (such as the five points of the Gospel: God, humanity, Christ, the answer, new creation) and how to respond to common objections.

2. Organizing regular evangelistic outreaches – In parks, plazas, markets, and door-to-door, distributing Bible literature and engaging in respectful conversations.

 3. Develop community outreach events – Outdoor Bible classes, service fairs, film screenings followed by discussions, and Q&A nights where Christ is proclaimed without shame.

4. Accompany new converts – Connecting them with personal discipleship and a local church where they can be integrated, because evangelism doesn't end with the decision.
