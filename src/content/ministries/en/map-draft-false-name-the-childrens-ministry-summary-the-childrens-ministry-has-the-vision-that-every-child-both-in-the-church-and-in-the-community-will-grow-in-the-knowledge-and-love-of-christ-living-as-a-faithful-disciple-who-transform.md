Vision and Work of the Children's Ministry

"To see every child in our church and community grow in the knowledge and love of Jesus Christ, living as faithful disciples, secure in God's sovereign grace, and transforming their surroundings with the power of the Gospel, for His glory, who is the answer to every need."

Biblical Foundation: Proverbs 22:6; Psalm 145:4; Matthew 19:14

Objective (Mission) of the Children's Ministry

"To guide children and their families toward a genuine and reformed faith in Christ through expository Scripture teaching, constant prayer, intentional discipleship, and genuine affection, so that every child recognizes that Jesus alone is the answer for their life, repents of their sins, trusts in Him, and lives to honor God in every area of ​​their life."

 Specific actions that reflect the objective:

1. Faithfully teach the Bible – Presenting Christ throughout Scripture, with age-appropriate discipleship, affirming the doctrine of grace and the authority of the Word.

2. Foster a personal and covenantal faith – Helping children understand that salvation is by grace through faith, and that they are part of God's people from this moment forward.

3. Empower parents – Equipping families as the primary guardians of spiritual upbringing, according to Deuteronomy 6.

4. Create a warm and safe environment – ​​Where every child feels loved, valued, and challenged to follow Jesus in community.

5. Promote service and witness – Forming young servants who, from childhood, share with their friends and neighbors that Jesus is the answer.
