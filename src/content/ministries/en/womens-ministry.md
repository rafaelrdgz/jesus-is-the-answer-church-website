Women's Ministry

Objective: The Women's Ministry's primary purpose is to strengthen the spiritual, family, and community life of the women of the church, promoting their growth in the knowledge of Christ and its practical application in every area of ​​their lives. It seeks to be a space for encounter, mutual edification, and service, where each woman discovers and develops the gifts God has given her to bless her family, the church, and society.

Mission: We are a ministry that, grounded in the Word of God and under the guidance of the Holy Spirit, strives to:

• Disciple and teach women in Reformed doctrine, helping them to live according to biblical principles.

• Foster fellowship and support among women, creating bonds of genuine sisterhood that reflect the love of Christ.

• Mobilize active service within and outside the congregation, especially toward those most in need, as a living expression of the gospel.  • To support families by empowering women in their roles (whether as wives, mothers, single women, or widows) according to biblical principles, so that they may be agents of transformation in their homes and communities.

Our desire is that every woman may say, "I am the servant of the Lord," and that her life may be a true reflection that Jesus is the answer.
